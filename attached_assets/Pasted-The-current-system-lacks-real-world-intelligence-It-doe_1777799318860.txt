The current system lacks real-world intelligence. It does not use real-time geopolitical, weather, or disruption data, and fails to explain *why* a route is dangerous.

You must upgrade the platform into a **live intelligence-driven routing system** with explainable risk insights.

---

## Core Objective

Integrate **real-time external data sources** and build a UI that clearly explains:

* WHY a route is dangerous
* WHAT is causing the risk
* HOW the system is predicting disruption

This is NOT just data display — this is **decision intelligence**.

---

## 1. Real-Time Data Integration (MANDATORY)

Integrate live data from:

### Geopolitical / News Data

* Fetch real-time global news (conflicts, wars, trade disruptions)
* Use APIs (e.g., GDELT, NewsAPI, or similar)

Detect:

* War zones
* Trade route blockages
* Political instability

Example:

* Strait of Hormuz conflict → flag as CRITICAL risk

---

### Weather Data

* Integrate live weather APIs
* Detect:

  * Storms (sea)
  * Cyclones
  * Heavy rainfall (land)
  * Turbulence (air)

---

### Traffic / Logistics Data

* Road congestion (for land)
* Port congestion (for sea)
* Airspace restrictions (for air)

---

## 2. AI Risk Detection Layer

Build a risk engine that:

* Combines:

  * News signals
  * Weather data
  * Route location

* Outputs:

  * Risk score (0–100)
  * Risk type (weather / war / congestion)
  * Severity (Low / Medium / High / Critical)

---

## 3. Explainable Risk UI (VERY IMPORTANT)

When a route is flagged:

Display a **detailed explanation panel**:

### Must include:

* 🚨 Risk Title:
  Example:
  "Critical Risk: Conflict near Strait of Hormuz"

* 📍 Affected Region (highlight on map)

* 🧠 AI Explanation:
  Example:
  "Recent geopolitical escalation detected from multiple news sources indicating potential disruption to oil shipping lanes."

* 📰 Related News Articles:

  * Show headlines
  * Show source
  * Clickable links

* 🖼 Optional Visual Context:

  * Images from news (if available)

* 🌦 Weather Impact (if applicable)

---

## 4. Map Integration (Live Risk Overlay)

Enhance map with:

* Risk zones:

  * Red (Critical)
  * Orange (High)
  * Yellow (Moderate)

* Clicking a zone → opens full explanation panel

* Animate or highlight dangerous regions

---

## 5. Live Alerts System

Create a **real-time alert feed**:

* Show:

  * New risks detected
  * Escalating threats

Example alerts:

* "⚠️ Storm forming in Bay of Bengal — potential delay"
* "🚨 Conflict escalation near Strait of Hormuz — rerouting advised"

---

## 6. AI-Based Prediction (NOT JUST REACTIVE)

System must:

* Predict risks BEFORE they happen
* Use:

  * Trend analysis from news frequency
  * Weather forecasts

Example:

* Increasing conflict mentions → predict route instability

---

## 7. Rerouting Intelligence

When risk detected:

* Automatically:

  * Suggest safer alternative route
  * Show comparison:

    * Old route risk
    * New route safety
